Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 5, 2025  ·  Last updated: May 5, 2025

Overview

Elitr ("Elitr," "we," "our," or "us") operates the travel intelligence platform available at app.elitr.ai and the marketing website at elitr.ai. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and share information about you when you use our services.

Elitr is built on the principle that your data should work for you — not against you. We collect only what is necessary to deliver Sol's intelligence, and we do not sell your personal information.

What we collect

Information We Collect

We collect information in two contexts: when you interact with the Elitr marketing site, and when you use the Elitr app.

Data type
How and why we collect it
Email address
Collected when you join the waitlist or create an account. Used for account authentication, transactional emails, and platform communications. We do not use your email for marketing without your consent.
Financial account data
Connected via Plaid when you link a financial institution. Includes account balances, transaction history, and institution identifiers. Used exclusively to power Sol's portfolio intelligence — understanding your spending patterns, card utilization, and earning opportunities. We do not store raw transaction data beyond what is necessary to generate and refresh Sol's context.
Loyalty program data
Connected via AwardWallet when you authorize the integration. Includes program names, point and mile balances, and status levels. Used to give Sol an accurate picture of your rewards portfolio and to surface time-sensitive redemption opportunities.
Travel preferences and trip context
Information you provide directly to Sol — destinations, travel dates, cabin preferences, hotel preferences, and other context you share in conversation. Used to personalize Sol's recommendations and to build your memory brief, which persists between sessions to improve Sol's understanding of your goals over time.
Conversation data
Messages and context you share with Sol are processed by Anthropic's API to generate responses. This data is transmitted to Anthropic for inference. Please review Anthropic's privacy policy for information on how they handle this data.
Usage data
Standard technical information including device type, operating system, and interaction patterns within the app. Used to diagnose issues, improve the platform, and understand how features are used. We do not build advertising profiles from this data.

Use of data

How We Use Your Information

All data collected is used to deliver, improve, and secure the Elitr platform. Specifically:

To power Sol. Your financial data, loyalty balances, travel preferences, and conversation history are assembled into Sol's context window so she can reason about your actual situation — not a generic traveler's situation. This context is updated periodically and stored in your memory brief.

To authenticate your account. Your email address is used to issue magic links and one-time sign-in codes. We do not store passwords.

To send transactional communications. We send emails related to your account activity — sign-in codes, access confirmations, and platform notifications. We do not send marketing emails without your explicit consent.

To improve the platform. Aggregated, anonymized usage patterns help us understand what is working and what needs improvement. We do not use individual user data for this purpose without anonymization.

We do not sell your data. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for any purpose, including advertising.

Third-party services

Data Processors and Partners

Elitr uses the following third-party services to deliver the platform. Each receives only the data necessary for their specific function.

Plaid
Financial data connectivity. Plaid facilitates secure connections to your financial institutions and transmits account and transaction data to Elitr. Plaid's use of your data is governed by their Privacy Policy at plaid.com/legal.
AwardWallet
Loyalty program data aggregation. AwardWallet retrieves your points and miles balances from participating loyalty programs. Their use of your data is governed by their Privacy Policy at awardwallet.com.
Anthropic
AI inference. Sol's responses are generated by Anthropic's Claude API. Conversation content — including messages you send to Sol and the context assembled from your portfolio data — is transmitted to Anthropic for processing. Anthropic's data practices are described at anthropic.com/privacy.
seats.aero
Award flight availability. Travel search queries — including origin, destination, travel dates, and cabin class — are transmitted to seats.aero to retrieve real-time award availability. seats.aero's privacy practices are described at seats.aero.
Resend
Transactional email delivery. Your email address is transmitted to Resend to deliver sign-in codes and account communications. Resend does not use this information for any purpose beyond delivery.
Upstash
Session and memory storage. Your session tokens, memory brief, and persistent app data are stored in Upstash's Redis-compatible key-value store, keyed to your authenticated email address. Data is stored in the United States.
Vercel
Hosting and serverless functions. Elitr's front end and API functions are hosted on Vercel's infrastructure. Standard server logs may capture IP addresses and request metadata. Vercel's data practices are described at vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy.

Artificial intelligence

AI-Generated Content

Sol's responses — including recommendations, alerts, analysis, and conversational replies — are generated by artificial intelligence. Specifically, Sol is powered by Anthropic's Claude API.

Sol's intelligence compounds over time through your memory brief, which stores context about your portfolio, preferences, and travel goals between sessions. This memory is assembled by Elitr and injected into Sol's context with each conversation.

Sol does not make financial decisions on your behalf. All recommendations are informational and intended to support your own decision-making. Elitr is not a licensed financial advisor, and Sol's outputs should not be construed as financial advice.

Retention and deletion

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. Specific retention practices:

Session tokens expire after a short inactivity window and are automatically purged from storage.

Memory briefs persist between sessions to support Sol's contextual intelligence. You may request deletion of your memory brief at any time by contacting us.

Financial and loyalty data is retained only as long as your integration is active. If you disconnect Plaid or AwardWallet, we delete the associated data from our systems within 30 days.

Account deletion. If you request account deletion, we will remove your data from our active systems within 30 days. Residual data in backup systems will be purged on the next scheduled backup cycle.

Your rights

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information. You may also withdraw consent for data integrations (Plaid, AwardWallet) at any time by disconnecting them in the app.

If you are a resident of California, the European Union, the United Kingdom, or another jurisdiction with applicable privacy legislation, you may have additional rights including the right to know what data we hold, the right to portability, and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

Age restriction

Children's Privacy

Elitr is not directed at or intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that a minor has provided us with personal information, we will delete that information within 1 business day.
